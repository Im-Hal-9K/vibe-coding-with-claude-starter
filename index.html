This is your workspace. You are about to build something real, and you do not have to write the code, you describe what you want and Claude writes it.

1. Open the Claude panel (or run claude in the terminal) and say hello.
2. Tell it what you want to build. Start with the example prompt your instructor gives you, or pick one from PROMPTS.md.
3. Run it, look at it, and ask Claude to change something. That loop, describe, look, refine, is the whole skill.

When you build your own page, it replaces this one. Have fun.
