# Pick one and build it

Each is a single-page web app Claude can build and run right here, all genuinely useful or fun, all yours to keep. Paste the **starter** into the Claude panel to get a working version fast, then use the **iterate** prompts to practice directing the AI.

---

## 1. Personal page / link hub
A one-page site with your name, a short bio, a photo, and links to share.

**Starter:** "Build me a clean one-page personal website with my name 'Pat Smith' as a big heading, a short bio paragraph, and three buttons that link to my LinkedIn, my email, and my website. Make it look modern and friendly."

**Iterate:**
- "Add a photo at the top, round, centered."
- "Change the colors to navy and gold."
- "Make it look good on a phone too."

---

## 2. A calculator you'd actually use
Tip/bill splitter, savings goal, loan payment, or a unit/recipe converter.

**Starter (tip splitter):** "Build a tip calculator: I type the bill amount, pick a tip percent with buttons (15/18/20/25), and say how many people are splitting it. Show the tip, the total, and the amount per person. Make it big and easy to tap."

**Iterate:**
- "Let me type a custom tip percent too."
- "Add a 'round up to the nearest dollar per person' toggle."
- "Make the result update the instant I change anything."

---

## 3. A tracker
Habits, water, workouts, or a reading list. Saves in your browser so it's there next time.

**Starter:** "Build a daily habit tracker. I can add habits, check them off each day, and see a row of the last 7 days for each habit. Save everything in the browser so it remembers when I come back."

**Iterate:**
- "Show me a little streak counter for each habit."
- "Let me delete a habit I don't want anymore."
- "Add a satisfying check animation."

---

## 4. A study tool
Flashcards or a quiz on anything you're learning.

**Starter:** "Build a flashcard study app. I can add cards with a question and answer, then flip through them one at a time, clicking to reveal the answer. Save my cards in the browser."

**Iterate:**
- "Add a shuffle button."
- "Let me mark a card 'got it' or 'review again' and show me the review ones first."
- "Show 'card 3 of 12' so I know where I am."

---

## 5. Fun-but-real
An event countdown, a decision spinner, or a tiny game.

**Starter (countdown):** "Build a countdown page to a date I choose, showing days, hours, minutes, and seconds ticking down, with a title like 'Vacation in:'. Make it look celebratory."

**Iterate:**
- "Let me change the date and title without editing code."
- "Add a fun message and confetti when it hits zero."
- "Let me pick a background color theme."

---

## The moves that work on anything
- **Add:** "Add a [thing] that does [behavior]."
- **Change:** "Change the [color/layout/wording] to [what]."
- **Fix:** "It's doing [wrong thing] but I want [right thing]." (or paste the error)
- **Explain:** "What does this part do?"
- **Polish:** "Make it look more [modern / playful / professional]" and "make it work on a phone."
